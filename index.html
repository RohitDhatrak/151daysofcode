FAQ

Personal projects, solving problems from sites like codewars, gfg basically anything that involves one hour of writing code.


Q) How to keep track of the time you spend writing code?

You can use Wakka Time a website that keeps track of the time you spend writing code. It supports all the major IDEs

You need to do either one of them as a part of the challenge.
