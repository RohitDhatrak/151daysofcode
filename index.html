Ready for the #151daysofcode challenge?

This challenge is about developing a habit to code consistently and putting out content to make your self visible in the community.

Rules

Code

Minimum one hour of coding for 151 days

Tweet

Tweet about what you have done at the end of the day.

Blog

Or write a weekly blog summarizing what you did throughout the week.

Prizes

  • Five people who complete the challenge are going to win prizes from Tanay! In order to be eligible for the prize you have to code for straight 151 days starting from 1st Jan 2021.
  • Tweet about it everyday and use the hashtags #151daysofcode and #neogcamp.
  • Or write a blog every week summarizing what you learned throught the week and share it on social media and the discord server.
  • If you don't code for 151 days straight you won't lose the challenge. You can still complete the challenge within 201 days. But you won't be eligible for the prize.

FAQ

What counts in the 1 hour of code?

Personal projects, solving problems from sites like codewars, gfg anything that involves one hour of writing code.

Do I need to tweet or write a blog?

You need to do either one of them as a part of the challenge.
